Credit Requirements

1. The minimum total credits requirements are 40 credits, among them at least 20 credits for degree courses, at least 8 credits for non-degree courses, and 12 credits for social practice.

2. All courses are normally required to be completed within 1 year.

3. Degree courses are subject to this course plan, elective courses can be chosen from “Graduate Courses Catalog”.

4. Social practice

      Each student must practice at least 3 major branches in teaching base, such as communicable disease control, chronic diseases control, environmental and occupational health, health management, health supervision, health promotion, inspection and quarantine, environmental protection and monitoring, work safety supervision. The practice time in each department should be not less than 4 weeks and the total time should be at least 6 months. Through listening to introduction and field practice, as well as conversation and discussion with the staff in the teaching base and field work participation, the students should understand the institutional system, work responsibilities and tasks, management situation and services demand of teaching base. At same time, the student should combine with the field practice to analyze and discuss the urgent public health-related issues. At the end of the practice in each department, the student should prepare a summary and fill out social practices evaluation form. Qualified students will finish 12 credits.
